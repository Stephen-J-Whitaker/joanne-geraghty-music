About Jo

Jo is a singer songwriter who is passionate about her music. She has been writing her own songs since she was in her early teens and plays the guitar.

Jo regularly performs her music at gigs for the public and private events both on her own and with Bridget McMahon in and around the Connacht area in Ireland.
